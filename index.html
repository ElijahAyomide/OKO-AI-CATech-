Vision: To be Africa’s leading institute in smart agriculture, renewable energy and agribusiness innovation.
Mission: To train and certify young Africans with skills to apply AI, IoT and renewable energy technologies for sustainable agriculture and rural development.

School of Agricultural Technology

The School of Agricultural Technology is the foundation of OKO AI CATech, designed to equip students with strong practical and scientific knowledge in crop and animal production systems. It emphasizes sustainable, climate-smart agriculture, precision farming, and integration of AI-driven tools for yield optimization.

School of Agribusiness & Value Chains

The School of Agricultural Technology is the foundation of OKO AI CATech, designed to equip students with strong practical and scientific knowledge in crop and animal production systems. It emphasizes sustainable, climate-smart agriculture, precision farming, and integration of AI-driven tools for yield optimization.

School of Agro-Processing & Mechanization

This school drives value addition & postharvest efficiency through mechanization and agro-processing technology. It emphasizes hands-on technical training in equipment operation, maintenance, and small-scale factory setup — linking production to market-ready products

School of Smart Farming & Renewable Energy

This school integrates Internet of Things (IoT), Artificial Intelligence (AI), and renewable energy solutions into agriculture. It focuses on creating a new generation of farmers skilled in deploying smart devices, sensors, and clean energy technologies for efficient production.
